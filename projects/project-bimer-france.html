BIMer Services France

About BIMer Services:

BIMer Services provides an innovative Management, Operation, and Maintenance (GEM) solution powered by digital modeling (BIM). This cutting-edge approach simplifies and optimizes building management in compliance with ISO 16739, ensuring interoperability and independence from proprietary software. The digital twin offers structured, mobile access to all essential information related to your real estate portfolio. This enables cost-effective asset management—reducing operational expenses by up to 5%—while simultaneously increasing the value and longevity of your assets. Our solutions are tailored to support multiple domains, including: Asset Management, Project Management, Rental and Lease Management, Daily Facility Operations, Financial Oversight. By integrating these disciplines, BIMer Services delivers a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for holistic real estate and facilities management.

Project Details

  • Location: Montpellier, France
  • Duration: 2022 - 2023
  • LOD: 300
  • Role: Revit Architecture BIM Modeler
  • Worked Firm: HSS BIM Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
  • Tools Used: Revit, IFC Viewer, Dynamo (basic usage), AutoCAD,MS Excel, MS Word
  • Reference: Project Reference

Key Contributions

  • Developed as-built Revit models for residential and commercial buildings, including architectural and MEP components.
  • Used Dynamo for automating sheet creation, schedule creating, place the schedules with respective sheets and quantity takeoffs, improving efficiency.
  • Check the data match with standards to ensure model accuracy.
  • Ensured IFC-compliant models for seamless data exchange with French stakeholders.

Challenges and Solutions

Adapting to French building standards required extensive coordination. I conducted thorough reviews with local teams and used AutoCAD for 2D drafting to align with client requirements, ensuring model compliance.

Outcomes

The accurate as-built models supported facility management and renovation planning, delivering value to clients and meeting project deadlines.
