I am a Senior BIM Modeler with 3 years of experience at HSS BIM Solutions PVT.LTD., specializing in Civil 3D and Revit for overseas infrastructure projects. I have modeled complex utility networks (water supply, sewage, drainage, electrical, telecom, and bridges) and developed parametric Revit families for infrastructure components, adhering to BEP standards. What I Do

BIM & Modeling Services

Utility Network Modeling

Parametric Family Creation

Developing parametric Revit families for bridges, drainage structures, and streetlights, adhering to BEP standards for infrastructure projects.

Clash Detection & Resolution
